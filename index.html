About

I'm an NYC based product manager working at Fluent, where I'm currently working on crafting engaging content experiences. I'm a strong believer in superior user experience as a competitive advantage, and I use hypothesis-driven A/B testing to drive products closer to providing the optimal experience.

Outside of work, you can find me ogling watches, playing Fortnite, or working on projects like this site. Please get in touch if you want to talk product, watches, or anything else. I'm always interested in what you're working on!
